3 Concrete Steps to Skyrocketing Your Portfolio Today

By SEAN O'GRADY and AISHA NOZARI FOR MAILONLINE
PUBLISHED: 15:50 BST, 1 June 2021 | UPDATED: 16:14 BST, 1 June 2021

If you’re wondering where to invest, how much to invest, or how to go about it, you’ve come to the right place. During this article, we’re going to provide you with all the tools you need to fast-track your investments. We’ll keep it brief: Time is money, and you’ll be seeing plenty of that once we’re done here.

Have you ever been looking for something you thought you’d lost, only to discover that you were holding it in your hand the entire time?

I remember one time, I was sure I had laid my glasses down somewhere. I just couldn’t remember where! I searched high and low for them: on the tabletop, by the computer, on the side… then it struck me – I had been wearing them all along! How embarrassing!

It just goes to show that sometimes we spend time looking for things, when in fact, the thing we are looking for is right in front of our nose.

When it comes to investments, the same logic applies. Maybe you’ve been trawling the internet trying to find the next ‘Apple’; the new Bitcoin, so that you can get in early. Wouldn’t it be great to watch smugly as everybody jumps on the bandwagon after you – because you made the shrewd decision to get in early?

Don’t get me wrong, that strategy can work. However, it’s risky – you could lose it all. It’s also extremely difficult to predict which start-ups are going to blow up… after all, if it was easy, everyone would be doing it, and they wouldn’t be small start-ups anymore!

Okay, okay, I hear you: enough about what not to do. Let’s get into the step-by-step of how to execute on proper investments and start earning big.

1. Take advantage of the big boys!

As we’ve established, the best opportunities are sometimes right in front of your nose. Companies that might seem like an obvious choice – such as Amazon - are the perfect opportunity, and here’s why:

Firstly, Amazon has been booming during the pandemic. Where other businesses have struggled, Amazon has shown time and again that it has the resilience to thrive even during difficult times. Despite the pandemic, their share price has soared from around $1900 at the start of 2020 to around $3600 in July 2021!

This speaks volumes, and it means that those looking to make a shrewd investment by jumping on board the Amazon train need to do so urgently. The sooner you can get in, the more you’re likely to make… everybody is catching on to the growing momentum that Amazon is building. Don’t miss out!

What was doubly great about this for Amazon was that they continued to deliver the same great service to their customers without raising their prices - at a time where consumers would likely have been willing to pay more.

This wasn’t by luck, either. Amazon demonstrated typically shrewd business strategy by hiring 500,000 people globally and paying them well. Most importantly, perhaps, they spent $2 billion every quarter on COVID-related expenses, such as paid time off for employees. Quality practices such as these ensure that Amazon remains well esteemed as a company.

Furthermore, Amazon Prime increased by 50 million members since the start of 2020. As consumers became more reluctant (and unable) to visit in-person stores, they flocked to perhaps the greatest, fastest online delivery service available: Amazon Prime.

And to top it all off, Jeff Bezos wrote a letter to shareholders saying that the Amazon Web Service segment that made $45 billion in 2020 was on course to $50 billion soon. It’s an absolute no-brainer!
